# JACI Vision

## The Home of Knowledge Creators

### Our Mission

JACI is a social platform designed for people who create value through knowledge, ideas and contribution.

Unlike traditional social networks that prioritize popularity and attention, JACI promotes recognition, collaboration and meaningful impact.

We believe that talent exists everywhere.

Recognition should too.
